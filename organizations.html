The problem

Your team is doing the work. The outcomes are hard to prove.

Every grant renewal, board meeting, and agency partnership depends on clear employment data. Most programs still track resumes, applications, interviews, and retention across spreadsheets, notes, emails, and individual case files.

Disconnected tracking Case managers may know client progress, but directors cannot see the full employment pipeline in one place.
Slow reporting Grant and board reports take hours or days to compile, and the numbers can be stale by the time they are submitted.
Missed retention milestones 30, 60, and 90 day check ins are easy to miss without a simple follow up queue.
Privacy sensitive population Programs need outcome visibility without over collecting sensitive personal or conviction history data.
