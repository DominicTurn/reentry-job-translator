Built for reentry, workforce, and community programs

Many people returning home have real work experience, but that experience is often hard to explain on a traditional resume. Kitchen work, sanitation, laundry, maintenance, clerical work, canteen operations, orderly roles, and facility jobs all build transferable skills.

ReEntry Job Translator helps turn that experience into professional language without exaggerating credentials or hiding the truth.

How organizations can use it

Resume Workshops

Use the tool during job-readiness classes to help participants quickly create stronger resume bullets.

Case Management

Help clients identify transferable skills and prepare for employment conversations.

Workforce Programs

Support job seekers with practical language they can use for applications, interviews, and career pathways.

Privacy-first by design

ReEntry Job Translator does not require users to create an account. The tool is designed for simple, no-login access so individuals can get help without unnecessary barriers.

  • No account required
  • No payment required for individuals
  • No resume database
  • Built to support dignity, privacy, and employment readiness
